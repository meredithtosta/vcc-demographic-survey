System Architecture

Two-Tier Data Separation

  • Tier 1 - Operational Data: Aggregated counts only, no individual identifiers
  • Tier 2 - Compliance Data: Encrypted individual responses, access-logged

Compliance Features

  • Automatic anonymization at collection point
  • 5-year retention of encrypted responses
  • Audit trail for all access to individual responses
  • Survey links prevent founder identification
